Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

School Max ("we", "us") operates schoolmax.vercel.app. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account info: When you sign in with Google or create an account via Firebase Authentication, we store your email address and user ID to manage your account and subscription.

Writing samples: If you provide a voice sample, it is stored in our database and used solely to personalize AI-generated output to match your writing style.

Generated content: Text you generate or paste into School Max is processed through third-party AI APIs (Anthropic, Groq, Undetectable.ai, Winston AI) to provide our services. We do not permanently store your generated content on our servers.

Google Docs access: If you connect Google Docs, we request read and write access only to documents you explicitly select or provide. We do not access any other files in your Google account.

Payment info: Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to provide School Max's features: generating text, humanizing text, checking AI detection scores, drip-writing to Google Docs, and managing your subscription. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate School Max:

Firebase (authentication and database), Stripe (payments), Anthropic and Groq (AI generation), Undetectable.ai (humanizer), Winston AI (AI detection), and Google APIs (Docs integration).

Data Retention

Your account data and writing samples are retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
